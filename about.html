About Us

The Needham High School Rocketry Club is an excellent opportunity for students at NHS to get involved in STEM and learn about rockets. We aim to compete in the Team America Rocketry Challenge, a rocketry competition for high school students, and to provide an environment for our members to learn more about rocketry.


We meet Mondays and Thursdays after school in room 209. If you want to stop by, contact us first to make sure that we're not out launching rockets! Most of our launches will take place at Claxton Field, though we may attend other events in the area.
